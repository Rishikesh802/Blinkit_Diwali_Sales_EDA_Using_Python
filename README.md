# Blinkit Diwali Sales EDA using Python

## Project Overview
This project focuses on performing Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on Blinkit's Diwali sales dataset to understand customer purchasing behavior, sales trends, and product performance during the festive season. The analysis helps businesses make data-driven marketing and sales decisions.

---

## Problem Statement
Blinkit wanted to understand customer demographics and purchasing patterns during Diwali sales to improve customer targeting, product strategy, and overall sales performance.

---

## Objectives
- Perform data cleaning and preprocessing on sales data.
- Conduct Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) to identify customer trends.
- Analyze purchasing behavior based on gender, age group, occupation, marital status, and state.
- Identify top-performing product categories and regions.
- Generate actionable business insights using visualizations.

---

## Dataset Information
The dataset contains customer and sales-related information such as:
- Customer Gender
- Age Group
- State
- Occupation
- Marital Status
- Product Categories
- Number of Orders
- Sales Amount

## EDA Performed
- Gender-wise Sales Analysis
- Age Group Analysis
- State-wise Sales Analysis
- Occupation Analysis
- Marital Status Analysis

- Top Customers Analysis

---

## Key Insights
- Female customers contributed more to overall sales compared to male customers.
- Customers aged between 26–35 years showed the highest purchasing behavior.
- Uttar Pradesh, Maharashtra, and Karnataka generated the highest sales.
- Married women working in IT, Healthcare, and Aviation sectors were among the top buyers.
- Food, Clothing, and Electronics were the most purchased product categories.

---

## Business Conclusion
Married women aged 26–35 years from Uttar Pradesh, Maharashtra, and Karnataka working in IT, Healthcare, and Aviation sectors are more likely to purchase products from Food, Clothing, and Electronics categories during the Diwali season.
